#01 / End-to-End Ownership

MaaS Platform: Churn Insight Engine & Feature Delivery

Phased Launch

The Challenge

Identified a critical data gap where monthly user churn lacked qualitative feedback. The goal was to transform "blind churn" into actionable insights without overwhelming the engineering pipeline.

Execution & Results

  • Strategic Planning: Designed a phased approach (Phase 1: Diagnostic) to validate hypotheses before full-scale feature development.
  • Cross-functional Scoping: Collaborated with designers and engineers via Large Scale Scrum (LeSS) to implement a "low-effort, high-impact" feedback loop.
  • Data-Informed ROI: Conducted commercial impact analysis and competitive research to define clear PRDs and backlog prioritization.
#02 / Navigating Ambiguity

Supply Chain Operations: Internal Asset & Inventory Management

Cross-border Coordination

The Challenge

Navigated a high-complexity project with frequent leadership changes and information silos. Tasked with bridging the gap between regional technical requirements (SG/SZ) and local operational realities.

Action & Leadership

  • Requirement Synthesis: Proactively reached out to stakeholders to consolidate fragmented requirements and document assumptions to realign the project roadmap.
  • Operational Logic: Deep-dived into complex inventory logic, covering everything from store asset transfers to consumable stock counting.
  • Testing & Quality: Led User Acceptance Testing (UAT) to ensure that technical outputs met the diverse needs of on-site store operations.
